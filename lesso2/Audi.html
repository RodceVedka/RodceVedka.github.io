Meteorological services help the aviation industry to reduce flight times, and fuel consumption. Weather change is increasingly, and it is taken into account in long-term planning because of it can influence air travel demand and destination choice.

An example of this are flights at high temperatures, as the temperature is higher, the air expands and has less density. It is thinner. That lower density reduces the amount of lift that is generated in the wings of an airplane, knowing that there is an upward temperature change, forecast from meteorological area helps them to make decition on their flights.

What is the importance of meteorology on Cattle Raising?

The weather directly affects livestock activity by acting on the productive physiology of the animal (milk, meat, eggs, hair, feathers) and indirectly by affecting forage development, fluctuation of the parasitic population, the microenvironment of the reception establishments of the storage of animals and food and the product marketing process.
